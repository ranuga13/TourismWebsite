Sri Lanka

Sri Lanka offers a unique blend of stunning landscapes, rich cultural heritage, and warm hospitality. From the golden beaches of the south coast to the lush tea plantations in the central highlands, there is something for everyone. Experience the flavorful cuisine, explore ancient temples and ruins, and encounter diverse wildlife

Hotels

Sri Lankan hotels range from luxurious five-star resorts to budget-friendly guesthouses. The majority of hotels are located in or near major cities and tourist hotspots such as Colombo, Kandy, Galle, and Nuwara Eliya. Many of these hotels offer stunning views of the sea, lush greenery, or ancient ruins.

Restaurants

Sri Lanka is a paradise for food lovers, with a rich culinary heritage that is deeply rooted in local traditions and influences from India, Portugal, the Netherlands, and the UK. As a result, the island offers a diverse range of restaurants that cater to different tastes and budgets.

Travel-related services

Tour operators and travel agencies offer a range of services to help visitors plan their itineraries, book flights, hotels, and tours, and arrange transportation during their stay. They can also provide information on popular tourist attractions, local customs, and cultural events.

Culture, History and Arts

Culture

Sri Lanka is a land of great cultural diversity. Religion pervades many aspects of life and constitutes a basic element of this diversity. Buddhist and Hindu temples, as well as mosques and churches, with their own colourful rituals, are the most readily visible features of the cultural landscape.

History

The history of Sri Lanka is intertwined with the history of the broader Indian subcontinent and the surrounding regions, comprising the areas of South Asia, Southeast Asia and the Indian Ocean. The early human remains found on the island of Sri Lanka date to about 38,000 years ago.

Arts

Sigiriya frescoes, Dambulla cave paintings and sculptures, and stone carvings of Anuradhapura and Polonnaruwa have earned their reputation worldwide. The Kandyan dancers, the folklore masks, handloom fabric, Beeralu lace making, and lacquer work in Sri Lanka are unique to the island.

Travel tips and advice

Tips on how to travel more efficiently, save money, and stay safe while travelling.

Travel more efficiently

Research your destination, create a packing list, and plan your itinerary. Download travel apps that can help you with everything from booking flights and hotels. Only bring essential items and try to pack them in a way that maximizes space and organization.

Save money

Set a budget: Create a realistic budget for your trip and stick to it. Travel during the off-season: Prices for flights, accommodations, and attractions are often cheaper during the off-season. Cook your own meals: Eating out can be expensive, so consider cooking your own meals.

Stay safe while travelling

Before you go, research your destination to get a sense of the local customs, laws, and potential safety issues. Keep your valuables secure. Avoid carrying too much cash. Pay attention to your surroundings, especially in crowded areas.
